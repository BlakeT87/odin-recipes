Sugar Cookies

Description

I’m so wild about this easy sugar cookie recipe, I may never make traditional ones again. In fact, these have been my go-to for the last couple of years. They’re infinitely easier and taste, well, just like a crunchy sugar cookie, and similar to shortbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 2/3 cup + 3 TBSP granulated sugar, divided
  • 2 cups + 1 TBSP all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional, but recommended)
  • choice of sprinkles (rainbow is the obvious choice)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Line 2 large baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. IN a medium bowl, using handheld electric mixer, beat together butter and 2/3 cup of sugar until combined.(it will be gritty, that's to be expected)
  3. Using a cookie scoop or spoon, roll the dough into 1-inch balls
  4. Gently roll the balls in the remaining 3 TBSP of sugar until lightly coated; transfer to the baking sheets 2 inches apart
  5. Using the bottom of a glass, press down on the balls to flatten. Sprinkle with sprinkles and lightly press down again to make them stick. (The dough should end up being about 1/4 inch thick. They will look small in diameter but they will spread a bit as they cook)
  6. Bake for 14-16 minutes or until slightly golden around the edges and on the bottom
  7. Remove from the oven and let rest on the baking sheets for at least 10-15 minutes (don't cheat!) then eat, or transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely
  8. ENJOY!
